My Journey

I started my journey in web development in Fall of 2022 by attending classes at Lakeshore Technical College. In Spring of 2023 I completed a Technical Diploma as a Web Development Specialist. At NWTC, I have been focusing more on frontend development and design. My strongest skills are HTML, CSS, Bootstrap and responsive design. At this time I am on track for graduation in the Spring of 2025.
